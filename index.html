    Who qualifies to register

    Adult candidates who are 21 years and older who: not registered for the Amended senior certificate examination before,provided they meet the following

    • A general education and training certificate( GETC ) or
    • A minimum of grade 9 or Standard 7
    • An incomplete senior certificate ( Old Curriculum )
    • An incomplete national senior certificate ( New Curriculum ) provided they wrote NSC exams during 2008 to 2011
    • Dropped out of school but are 18 to 21 years old. Head office must give approval first N.B To be awarded a senior certifacate candidate needs to pass a total of 6 subjects
    Professional Stuff
